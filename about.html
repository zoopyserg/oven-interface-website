A SMART OPEN SOLUTIONABOUT OUR SOLUTION

Industrial stoves play a major role in almost all industries.

This is a reliable expensive equipment that rarely breaks.

It is technically simple. There is almost nothing to break.

The main complexity of the stove is the interface, the capabilities of the stove depend on its capabilities. And often its price.

The only component that is almost impossible to replace in case of failure is the controller. Brains of the stove.

When there is a need to buy a new controller - production will rise, replacement will be expensive, and replacement will have to look for a long time and wait until the replacement arrives.

Our solution addresses these two weaknesses of industrial stoves.

It:

  • Is easy to install
  • Connects only easily accessible components which are easy to find a replacement at any time
  • Our interface has all the functions that meet modern conditions (temperature charts, modes, etc.)
